Doctor's Chart | Andy Robinson | March 2019



The linked chart is used for patients to record their data including Systolic and Diastolic Bloodpressures, their PulseOx, Glucose, and Wellness. This chart was created so if they are having any troubles, they will be aware and can contact help. Below are some charts that contain information on what is healthy and what isn't.
