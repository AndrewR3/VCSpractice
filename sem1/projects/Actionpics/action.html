How I made my Action Picture

To make this action picture, I waited until my dog (Oreo) was running toward me, and then I started taking a few pictures. I selected some of the best ones to doctor in GIMP to show every frame of Oreo in one pic. I layered the pictures as transparent and used the healing tool on the different layers to show as many Oreos as I could.






To make this gif, I opened all of the frames in GIMP as layers, and went to effect > animation > optimize for gif. Then I saved it as a gif format, and now it works
